Biography

Brooke is a PhD student in Psychology at Princeton University, where she is advised by Professors Uri Hasson and Thomas Griffiths. She's interested in characterizing the computational and neural basis for human intelligence—particularly in areas where Artificial Intelligence struggles, such as one-shot learning and language acquisition.

She originally hails from sunny Southern California; there she obtained her M.S. in Computer Science at UC Irvine and B.S. in Mathematics at UC San Diego. Prior to her graduate studies, Brooke had another career as a Software Engineer, she worked as a backend Java developer for Intuit, Blizzard Entertainment, and CBS Interactive.
